Privacy Policy

SolarSystemResource · a Corgan Studio spatial intelligence platform
Last updated: April 13, 2026

Overview

SolarSystemResource is operated by Corgan Studio ("we," "us," "our"). This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our heliocentric solar system spatial intelligence platform at solarsystemresource.com.

We are committed to minimal data collection. SolarSystemResource is a visualization platform, not a social network. We collect only what is necessary to provide the service.

Information We Collect

Account information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and password (hashed) for authentication purposes. Authentication is provided by Supabase.

Usage data: We collect anonymous usage statistics including page views and feature usage to improve the platform. We do not track individual browsing behavior across other sites.

Payment information: Subscription payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers or payment details on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy governs payment data handling.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use advertising trackers, behavioral profiling, or data brokers. We do not collect location data from your device.

Third-Party Services

SolarSystemResource integrates data from the following sources for visualization purposes:

  • NASA: Deep Space Network telemetry, mission ephemeris, JPL Horizons orbital data, NASA Tycho star catalog imagery
  • JPL Small Body Database: Asteroid, comet, and minor planet orbital elements
  • ESA: Deep space mission data
  • spacekit.js: Open-source 3D solar system rendering engine
  • Supabase: Authentication and database services
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Cloudflare: Content delivery and DDoS protection

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy.

Data Security

All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Authentication tokens are handled by Supabase with industry-standard security practices. API keys are rotated regularly and access-scoped.

Data Retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your account. You may request account deletion at any time by contacting contact@corganstudio.com. Anonymous usage statistics are retained indefinitely in aggregate form.

Cookies

SolarSystemResource uses minimal cookies for authentication session management only. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

Children's Privacy

SolarSystemResource is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact: contact@corganstudio.com
