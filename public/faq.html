Frequently Asked Questions

SolarSystemResource · a Corgan Studio, Inc. spatial intelligence platform
Last updated April 2026
What is Resource Systems?
Resource Systems is a multi-planetary spatial intelligence suite covering Earth, the Moon, Mars, the Sun, and every major body in the Solar System alongside a Milky Way galactic view. Each site in the suite shares a consistent design language and a unified account across bodies.
What is SolarSystemResource?
SolarSystemResource is A Solar System intelligence platform providing a single unified view of the Sun, planets, major moons, and interplanetary spacecraft in motion.
What can I see on the platform?
SolarSystemResource presents an interactive visualization of the Solar System with multiple selectable view layers and a consistent account and navigation shared across every Resource Systems platform.
Who created Resource Systems?
Jason Corgan Brown, founder of Corgan Studio, Inc. Corgan Studio is a research and design studio focused on spatial intelligence, worldbuilding, and large-scale visualization. Jason’s work spans spatial design for projects including NEOM, Amazon Studios, and the Obama Presidential Center, and creative leadership on cinematic franchises including Star Wars, Lord of the Rings, and Tron. Resource Systems launched in 2025.
Is there a paid plan?
Yes. A free tier is available alongside paid tiers for advanced features and higher usage. See the account menu inside the platform for current plans.
Is it available on mobile?
Yes. SolarSystemResource runs in modern desktop and mobile browsers. Some advanced 3D features perform best on desktop hardware.
